About


I was often curious about the fact that in this world of fashion and trends, who doesn't want a good piece of clothing? So I, Madhu and my friend Ritika thought to bring together our creativity and design garments exclusively for women. We formed or rather built up Marit Creations-an online boutique featuring trendy tops, kurtis, skirts and much more at affordable prices. We provide you with a wide variety of choices to choose from. We personally have made sure to provide you with best and unique designs stitched using best quality of fabric. We hope you like our collection. For any feedback both positive and negative, feel free to contact us. Go through our website and shop with us the latest trends curated just for you.
